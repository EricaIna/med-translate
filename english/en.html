About us

What is E-medical service?

We provide medical interpretation services for Japanese living in UK.
We offer a wide range of support for general problems related to medical care, from assistance at pharmacy to translation of English medical documents.

Point 1

All our staff member are medically qualified.
Experienced staff who have worked in a hospital in the UK will fully support you.

Point 2

Ready to help 24/7.
You can rest assured even in an emergency.

Service

Service features

Hospital accompaniment

We accompany and interpret for emergency departments (A&E) or General practitioners (GPs) at NHS hospitals (national hospitals) in the UK. It is safe to receive explanations, examinations, and prescriptions from doctors.
*Medical care in NHS hospitals is free.

Pharmacy correspondence

Assistance in drug selection at the pharmacy. Help you to engage with your local pharmacist and serve as an interpreter when needed. Even if you have many medical histories or allergies, you can choose medicine with confidence.
* Prescription drugs are subject to a fixed fee.

English translation of medical documents

We assist in translating various (all types of ) medical documents issued in Japan, including vaccination histories, maternal and child health handbooks, and health certificates for companies.
